MUNNAR

Munnar Hill Station

The popular Munnar hill station is located around 1500-1600 m above sea level. This hill station is found in the Western Ghats range of mountains of the Indian state of Kerala. Though Kerala is a place where tourists can avail the pool of natural beauties that are spread all over the state, if we talk about Hill Station then Munnar is the first choice that clicks in our mind.

EXPLORE MUNNAR

Exploring Munnar

The breathtaking beauty of Munnar hill station attracts a large number of people from all over the world. Visitors can take benefit of the pleasant climate and the tranquil atmosphere generate a heavenly feeling to the visitors. Munnar is often called as a tea town because visitors will be greeted with the everlasting tea plantations throughout Munnar. Apart from the tea plantations, Munnar also serves exotic species of flora and fauna, extravagant valleys and mountains. Visiting Kerala during monsoons is the perfect time for tourists and one can visit Munnar Hill Station from September to May also.

TEA GARDEN

Munnar Tea Garden

Munnar is well known for its tea plantations and a long walk in these beautiful tea gardens with your partner is the best way to spend your evening. Munnar is an ideal place where every kind of tourist can enjoy their trip by all means.
